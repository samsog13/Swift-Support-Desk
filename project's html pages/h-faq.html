Swiftie Knowledge Base

  • Frequently Asked Questions!

    Some common questions and answers. You might find what you're looking for here first.

    • Why does the Ticketmaster queue keep crashing during Eras Tour presale?

      High traffic during big sales can cause this. Try using the app, stay logged in,and avoid refreshing too often. If it still fails, create a ticket with your show details.

    • My merch or signed vinyl hasn’t arrived yet. What should I do?

      Shipping usually takes 2–4 weeks. Check your spam folder for tracking updates. If it’s been longer, create a ticket with your order number.

    • My presale code is not working. Is it expired?

      Most codes are one-time use and expire quickly. Make sure you're logged into the correct account. If it still doesn’t work, open a ticket.

    • Can I get a refund for my concert tickets?

      It depends on Ticketmaster’s policy. Most tickets aren’t refundable, but you can create a ticket if you have a special case.

    • How long does it take to get a reply?

      Usually within 24–48 hours. Urgent issues may get faster responses.
